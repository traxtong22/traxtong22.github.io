Kaleidoscope - Traxton Gordon EP

Welcome

This is the offical launch page for Traxton Gordon's first EP, titled Kaleidoscope. The EP is currently in production, and the page will be launched when the EP is finished. Kaleidoscope is a concept EP centered around dynamic sounds, and will feature accompanying visual elements.

Song #1 - Waves

Waves is the first song that was written for the EP, but will appear as the last recorded song. It serves as a gentle and contemplative conclusion to the collection.

Song #2 - Kaleidoscope

The title track of the EP, Kaleidoscope seeks to create a vast and well-rounded landscape with a variety of guitar and synth elements.

Song #3 - Right In Two (Cover)

A re-imagining of the classic TOOL song, Right In Two attempts to add a new perspective to the original masterpiece.
The Traxton Gordon EP is currently still being written and produced. Check back soon for the official launch.
